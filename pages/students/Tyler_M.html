Humuhumunukunukuapua'a fish (trigger fish)

The Humuhumunukunukuapua'a also known as the trigger fish is the hawaiin state fish.

The Humuhumunukunukuapua'a name when translated roughly means fish with a snout like a pig.

In the early Hawaiin days the people of hawaii would use dried Humuhumunukunukuapua'a as fuel for fires when cooking and that it would make a more flavorful food dish.
